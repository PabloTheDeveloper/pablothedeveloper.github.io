Purpose#

I have found writing to be an excellent tool for expression. Practically speaking, what one doesn't write one tends to forget. Writing further crystalizes understanding and serves as a cognitive tool to advance thinking. Really, these works save me time.

Lastly, this world has unbrimming amounts of knowledge and fascinations. It would be tragic to not capture some portion. By trade I am no writer but everyone has something to say and a life worth writing about.

Disclaimer#

I am personally writing all of these entries. No AI or AI tools will be used to author the contents contained here. Doing so would defeat the purpose of my writing. That being, mainly self expression, retaining knowledge, improving my thinking skills. AI would rob me of gains there.

A way to check, is to see that there are human errors here and there. A misplaced comma, missing word, bad grammar etc. AI has a place in many tedious functions of our world but when it comes to self expression, it would be disingenuous to levy AI. AI uses all of humanity's written artifacts to generate its thoughts. It would be akin to having someone else speak for me if I were to use it here.

Furthermore, I do not permit the usage of any of this content for AI training or for scraping. In the future, I'll add anti-bot and anti-scraping measures even if it's fruitless. I'll also change how I'm hosting this site as well. It is a sad reality that human authored public content is being feed to AI machines, mostly without consent. On principle I feel it important to have a disclaimer like this.
